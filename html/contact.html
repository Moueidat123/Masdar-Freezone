Frequently asked questions

A visit in Masdar City takes approximately 60-90 minutes. A walk through the City is low-level difficulty, and special-needs access is available.

Directions to Masdar City and the location map can be found here

Masdar City is open to the general public every day for those who wish to visit and explore at their own leisure.

Official delegations should book their visit via a booking form

There is parking available in the North Car Park. From there, you can access the city via shuttle service which operates from 6am to midnight daily.
